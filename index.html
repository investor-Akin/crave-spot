CraveSpot is a nationwide online platform that connects you with a wide variety of restaurants across Nigeria, delivering your favorite meals right to your doorstep quickly and conveniently.

We are proud to offer our delivery services nationwide across Nigeria. Simply enter your location during the ordering process to see the restaurants available in your area.

Ordering is easy!
1. Browse: Explore the extensive menus of restaurants near you.
2. Select: Choose the delicious meals you'd like to order and add them to your cart.
3. Checkout: Review your order, enter your delivery address, and choose your preferred payment method.
4. Enjoy: Sit back and relax while we deliver your food!

Our delivery hours vary depending on the specific restaurant you are ordering from. You can view the operating hours of each restaurant on their menu page. Generally, we offer delivery services throughout the day and evening.

Delivery fees vary based on your location and the distance from the restaurant. The applicable delivery fee will be clearly displayed at the checkout before you finalize your order. Keep an eye out for promotions, including free delivery for first-time users!

CraveSpot offers a variety of secure payment options, including online payments with your debit/credit card, bank transfers, and (where available) cash on delivery. You can select your preferred method at the checkout.

If you experience any issues with your order (e.g., incorrect items, missing food, late delivery), please contact our customer support team immediately through the "Contact Us" section on our website or app. We are committed to resolving any problems promptly.

Order cancellation policies may vary depending on the restaurant and the status of your order. Please check the cancellation options available in your order details or contact our customer support as soon as possible if you need to cancel.

We're always looking for great partners! If you're a restaurant owner interested in joining our platform or a driver looking for delivery opportunities, please visit the "Partners" section on our website and fill out the relevant form. Our partnerships team will get in touch with you.
