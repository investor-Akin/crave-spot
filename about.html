About CraveSpot

Delivering Nigeria's favorite meals with love and care.

Our Mission

At CraveSpot, our mission is simple yet powerful: to connect Nigerians with their favorite local restaurants, delivering meals that are fast, fresh, and made with love.

We believe that great food should be accessible to everyone, anywhere in Nigeria. That's why we've built a nationwide network of drivers and restaurant partners committed to bringing the best culinary experiences right to your doorstep.

Whether you're craving traditional Nigerian dishes or international cuisine, CraveSpot makes it easy to satisfy your hunger with just a few taps.

Our Story

CraveSpot was created by Onigbinde Akinbowale Simeon to revolutionize food delivery across Nigeria. After experiencing firsthand the challenges of getting quality food delivered efficiently, Simeon set out to build a solution that would bridge the gap between amazing restaurants and hungry customers.

What started as a small operation in SQI has quickly grown into a nationwide platform, connecting thousands of restaurants with customers in cities and towns across Nigeria.

Today, CraveSpot continues to innovate and improve, always focusing on our core values: speed, freshness, local flavor, and customer delight.
